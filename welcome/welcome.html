What info are you comfortable sharing with sites?



Track Your Info
This extension automatically records certain types of input you give to websites. If it cannot determine them, you can always manually track it through the pop-up window (click the extension icon).
View Trends in Your Data
Open the settings page to view both a list and a chart of your tracked info. Use this to better understand the type of info you share with sites!
